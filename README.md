Hardware components used for this project:

* An Arduino UNO r4 (Should work on any Arduino board with compatible pinout);
* 4 push buttons;
* 4 LED's being 1 blue, 1 green, 1 red and 1 yellow;
* 1 buzzer;
* 4 10 KΩ resistors;
* 5 100 Ω resistors;
* 1 100 nF capacitor;
